### Looking ahead

Next week, we begin working through the **consultancy cycle**: the stages of a typical consulting engagement from initial contact through to completion. Understanding this cycle will provide the structure for much of the rest of the module.

The stages we'll cover:
1. Entry and contracting
2. Discovery and diagnosis  
3. Analysis and solution development
4. Presentation and recommendations
5. Implementation support
6. Closure and evaluation

Week 1 has given you the context; the weeks ahead will develop the practical skills.
